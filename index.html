Frequently Asked Questions

Need help? Here are the answers to the most common questions about DevConf 2026.

How do I access the workshops?

All workshop registrations will be managed through our attendee portal. Standard ticket holders can select up to 2 sessions, while Pro and Team ticket holders get unlimited access to all interactive workshops.

Can I upgrade my ticket later?

Yes, absolutely! You can upgrade your ticket from Standard to Pro at any time before August 10, 2026, by paying the price difference. Simply log into your dashboard and click on "Upgrade Pass".

Will session recordings be available?

Yes, all main stage sessions and workshops are recorded. Standard pass holders will have digital access for 30 days post-event, while Pro and Team ticket holders will get lifetime access to the recordings.

Is there a hybrid or online option?

DevConf 2026 is strictly an in-person event to ensure high-quality networking, hands-on collaboration, and face-to-face mentorship. We do not support virtual attendance or online streaming this year.
